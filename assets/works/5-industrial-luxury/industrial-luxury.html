POSTERS: Industrial Luxury

Category Poster / Concept
Series Three Plates
Year 2026
Vibe High-Fashion Meets Industrial Grit

A series challenging the traditional boundaries between raw utility and elite luxury - staging the world's most recognisable fragrance house inside the gritty, unglamorous machinery of global freight.

Each plate pairs a brutalist, high-grain industrial setting with a single, precise luxury insignia, turning ordinary logistics scenery into a masterpiece of modern pop-surrealism.

1 — The Cargo Yard

A dramatic, high-grain black and white aerial shot of a sprawling train yard. Amid the gritty texture of rolling tanker cars and tracks, a massive shipping container is boldly branded - a striking juxtaposition of raw utility and elite luxury.

2 — The Water Tower

An unexpected monument to haute couture. A weathered, rustic water tower stands against a vast, grainy sky, stamped with an iconic luxury insignia - a witty, avant-garde elevation of everyday urban architecture.

4 — The Freight Yard

Power and elegance collide in a low-angle, high-contrast shot. A heavy-duty forklift hoists a massive container against stacked shipping crates - intense film grain turning a logistics scene into pop-surrealism.
